Burek

Description

This dish, of Turkish origin, is very popular in Croatia as a light meal which people buy to eat on the go.

Burek is filo pastry filled with seasoned meat.

Ingredients

  • filo-pastry
Filling:
  • 150g/5.5oz lean beef
  • 150g/5.5oz onion
  • 40g/1.5oz oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Fry the finely chopped onions and minced meat in oil.
  2. Add the salt and pepper.
  3. Grease a round baking tray and put a layer of pastry in it.
  4. Cover with a thin layer of filling and cover this with another layer of filo pastry which must be well coated in oil.
  5. Put another layer of filling and cover with pastry.
  6. When you have five or six layers, cover with filo pastry and bake at 200ºC/392ºF for half an hour.
  7. Cut in quarters and serve.
